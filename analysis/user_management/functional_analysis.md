**Password constraints**
			Passwords must contain:
			-   Passwords must be  at least 10 characters  in length
			-   a minimum of 1 letter [a-z] and
			-   a minimum of 1 letter [A-Z] and
			-   a minimum of 1 numeric character [0-9] and
			-   a minimum of 1 special character: "@#\-_$%^&+=ยง!\?"
